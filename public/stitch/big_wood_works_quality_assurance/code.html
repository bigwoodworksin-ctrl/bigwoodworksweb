Our Six-Stage Quality Process

From raw timber to final export, we employ meticulous checks at every transition point to eliminate defects and ensure longevity.

inventory_2

01. Material Inspection

Every batch of solid wood undergoes moisture checks to ensure levels are between 8-12%, preventing warping or cracking in various climates.

architecture

02. Process Control

Dimension verification occurs after every cut. We maintain a ±0.5mm tolerance for joinery to guarantee structural integrity and perfect assembly.

texture

03. Finish Review

Visual and tactile inspection of all surfaces. We check for grain consistency, sanding smoothness, and uniform application of non-toxic protective oils.

verified

04. Personalization Verification

For custom B2B orders, we perform lid closure checks and verify engraving accuracy against approved digital proofs to ensure brand alignment.

assignment_turned_in

05. Final Inspection

A comprehensive 50-point checklist covering hardware stability, edge softness, and overall aesthetic balance.

handshake

06. Buyer Approval Support

Transparency is key. We provide pre-shipment photo and video approval for every wholesale batch before they are securely packed.

Technical Rigor, Handcrafted Soul.

Our quality assurance is not just about catching mistakes; it's about honoring the heritage of woodworking through modern precision.

Moisture & Climate

India's climate differs vastly from European or North American markets. We utilize advanced kiln-drying and seasoning techniques followed by digital moisture tracking to ensure stability across global shipping lanes.

Fit & Function

For our signature wooden boxes and urns, lid closure checks are paramount. We test for pneumatic resistance—the gentle "sigh" of a perfectly fitted lid—ensuring an airtight and dignified seal.

Digital Transparency

Before any wholesale shipment departs, you receive a high-definition gallery and 360° video of random sample checks from your specific production run.

Hardware Integrity

All hinges, clasps, and linings are stress-tested. We use only premium-grade brass and stainless steel that resist tarnishing and maintain smooth operation for decades.
